# TD3 (Twin Delayed DDPG)

## 1) Goal
TD3 improves DDPG stability by addressing overestimation and noisy policy updates.

Main improvements over DDPG:
1. Twin critics (`Q1`, `Q2`) with clipped double-Q target.
2. Delayed actor updates (`policy_delay`).
3. Target policy smoothing noise.

## 2) Core target and update logic
Target action with smoothing:

`a_next = clip(mu_target(s_next) + eps, low, high)`

with clipped Gaussian `eps`.

Target value:

`y = r + gamma * (1 - done) * min(Q1_target(s_next, a_next), Q2_target(s_next, a_next))`

Actor update (delayed):

`L_actor = -mean(Q1(s, mu(s)))`

## 4) Important defaults
- Environment: `Pendulum-v1`
- Episodes: `160`
- `gamma=0.99`
- `tau=0.005`
- `policy_noise=0.2`
- `noise_clip=0.5`
- `policy_delay=2`

## 5) Strengths and risks
Strengths:
- More stable than DDPG in many continuous domains
- Better resistance to critic overestimation

Risks:
- More hyperparameters to tune than DDPG
- Can still be sensitive to reward scaling and budget choices
